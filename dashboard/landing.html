How it works

Four steps, one guarantee: nothing unverified survives.

A small, calibrated fact-checker scores each claim against the retrieved context. The score is honest; the threshold is yours to tune.

01

Retrieve

Hybrid BM25 + dense search pulls the supporting context.

→
02

Generate

The model answers from that context — and nothing else.

→
03

Verify

Each claim is scored for support by the evidence (0–1).

→
04

Correct

Claims below the threshold are dropped, or the answer abstains.

Why it works

The verifier separates grounded from invented.

Hallucinated sentences score low; grounded ones score high. That separation — measured across 17,213 sentences — is what the threshold cuts.
